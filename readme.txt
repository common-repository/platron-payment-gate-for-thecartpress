Platron payment gate for thecartpress

== Description ==

Platron is an electronic payment service that allows payments’ acceptance in online stores using popular online payment methods (bank cards, electronic wallet, mobile phones, Internet banking), as well as mass payouts.
Since 2009 Platron implemented many successful projects, 5000+ websites are in the partnership with Platron. Currently, the service allows integration of all the most popular electronic payment methods in Russia to any trading platform. The company takes the leading position in the market and offers its customers such services as:

- Accepting online payments on the site: 20+ payment methods
- Automated refunds
- Manual invoicing to the customer: payment ref. to be sent to the phone, email or to a messenger
- SMS alerts to customers about order’s status and payment
- Regular payments/ subscriptions / recurring payments. Can greatly facilitate the payment process and increase the loyalty of your customers
- Fraud monitoring
- Mass payouts service

== Installation ==

1. Upload plugin into wp-content/plugins
2. Activate and install the plugin Platron through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Register on www.platron.ru
4. Get and paste settings through the 'TCP Settings->Payment Gates->Platron'
